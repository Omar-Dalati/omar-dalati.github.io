Personal Profile

What do the results of these tests mean for you? After analysing the information regarding my test about myself I have noticed that there's a correlation between only two of them, the results of the Myers-Briggs personality test have determined that I am INFJ-T which stands for Introverted, Intuitive, Feeling and Judging. I am someone who thinks with their emotions more so than a logical perspective, however this doesn't mean I won't think logically whatsoever. I am principled and work within a manner of where I need to have a concise set of plans before I can get working, even though at the same time I am someone who is very hands on and loves to learn as they go which in my second test that I have chosen was Education Planner, the result that came from Education Planner was that I am someone who learns hands on, I learn when I am doing something, for example while creating my website, I code whatever I think looks the best to myself, and I continued to do so, however I still had a set layout within the back of my mind and worked on that basis before jumping in directly.
Honey and Mumford test stated I am a reflector which they reflect and prefer to stand back and observe before jumping into it. These are now two very different results from firstly me being hands on to now being reluctant and staying back. The truth is that I am someone who enjoys working with information that I have gathered prior and then going into whatever, it is I am going into, and I won't let myself stop.
