Project Idea

Overview: My project idea is that I want to create an app/website where you'll pay for your subscription services, such as Netflix, Amazon, and Spotify. Directly through my service carrier so that it reflects as one payment, not multiple payments, and all charges are aligned on the same day. Choose the subscription service you desire and add it to your account to be billed each month, and you can choose any service as you please. You will be able to manage all your subscriptions in one place.

Motivation: I am motivated by this project idea because I don't particularly appreciate having my subscriptions taken out every month at different times, and they are all separate invoices. So, we see this continuous build-up with services being charged monthly on bank statements. It isn't enjoyable! I want to create a service where all your subscriptions are captured within one payment at your billing date. It will ensure that you'll no longer have to encounter any more separate charges.

Description: My product/service is a subscription service where you can freely choose to add any of the subscriptions you use, whether that varies from having Netflix, Amazon, Crunchyroll, Stan, Disney+, or Spotify. See how I'm just listing and listing all these subscription-based services, and it's already getting quite annoying; I've just listed six services, which all have separate monthly charges and which get billed onto your bank account five times every month. You see that notification pop off on your phone. It gets annoying to manage, and what if you stop using one of those services? You'll log onto the provider's website and unsubscribe separately, one by one. Is it a nuisance to even consider having to do this right? So, what it is, I will create a service where you can manage all your subscriptions directly through your phone through our app and or our website. We will support whatever you use to the best of our ability. It is a straightforward system; you'll be able to search for the product you want to subscribe to, and you'll receive an invoice for your payments.

If you subscribe mid-month of your monthly cycle what will happen is, you'll be able to see how many days to go until your payment date. You'll only be required to pay the days counting to you when payment is required. So, you will not be paying anything extra for your bill, and then you'll have your payments lined up.

This service will be straightforward and accessible. Still, on a web browser which is a one-to-one copy, there are no differences or disadvantages to using the web browser. Using it on your phone is easier, but we want to ensure it is accessible to all! You'll be able to merge your existing accounts as we don't want you missing on your saved shows, it will automatically connect once your payment has gone through, and you'll be prompted to link up your account with your chosen subscription service! You won't be able to watch any services on our platform. The reasoning is that we are purely a subscription service for you to use the services, but all payments are directed through us.
