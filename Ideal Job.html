Ideal Job

A description of the position, and particularly what makes this position appealing to you Initially, I wanted to become a teacher at a primary school. I had one specific teacher in primary school who was my IT Teacher, and he was the one who got me interested in everything to do with technology. I genuinely do thank him for that. As I grew up, I told people I wanted to become an IT Teacher, and everyone discouraged me from doing so as they stated I wouldn't be a good fit. However, I took their words with a grain of salt. Now that being said, this position isn't a teaching job at all; however, it is a position that manages the School's ICT Environment, from Wi-Fi to the VOIP Telephony, security and the CCTV System. They do everything concerning IT within the school. It is what the position entails. But to be able to work for the staff and to assist students with any desktop/server/network/application issues is what makes me happy. I love being able to fix things and support others when they require my help.

A description of the skills, qualifications and experience required for the position. They require to have previous experience as a technical support engineer, an IT Help Desk type role, The ability to work with Microsoft servers, VMware, and Networking knowledge. There is no requirement for any studies; however, they want previous experience. They require previous work experience that ranges from Technical Support Engineer, Desktop Support Engineer, IT Help Desk Technician or similar role. There aren't any necessary qualifications for the position. However, the school requires previous work experience working with Microsoft Servers, services such as Active Directory DHCP, and DNS. They have demonstrated expertise in VMware or comparable virtualisation solutions, supporting and administering Windows and Apple Devices, working knowledge of VLANs and IP routing, Google apps suite administration, and experience with compass and School management systems. Understanding of audio-visual technologies. These are the technical requirements for the job. VLANs allow the students and teachers to have a direct network everywhere within the school network to manage security policies, internet traffic, and especially administration.
They also require someone to be able to explain technical situations to a non-technical audience, someone with excellent listening skills and time management, and they want someone who understands the college's needs and suggestions for improvements.

A description of the skills, qualifications and experience you currently have My current qualification is that I have a Certificate IV in Information Technology, in which I gained the skills to work with servers directly, experience using VMware and set up VLAN networks and IP Routing. I also have personal knowledge of working with Windows computers and apple devices such as iPhones, iMacs, MacBooks, and iPads. I have personal experience setting up my home cameras and routing the ethernet cables to the direct camera control system. However, I do not have any direct work experience in these areas.

How I will obtain the further required skills by finishing off my Bachelor of Information Technology, in which I plan to find an internship to develop my skills and gain the necessary skills required.
